Welcome to Crowdsource Rescue Net

Every now and then, when diaster strikes, the number of victims greatly exceed the number of rescuers. There are simply not enough emergency rescue teams who can help those in peril situations all at once. For that reason, our mission is to create a network to encourge victims to reach out to our network. In doing so, our rescue volunteers can be notified of nearby victims and can jump to their aid. This network will open up a community in which people can help those in need.
